Research Overview

Thesis proposal on modeling spatiotemporal land-use/land-cover change in the Pensacola metro area using GIS, remote sensing, and CA–Markov modeling.

LULC Change CA–Markov & Drivers Coastal Florida
  • Goal Quantify past LULC change and predict future urban growth under different scenarios.
  • Data Landsat-based LULC maps (2006, 2016, 2021) and driver variables (roads, distance to urban, coastline, population, etc.).
  • Methods Hybrid classification, CA–Markov, and driver evaluation to explain where, when, and why LULC change occurs.
